Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD - Move camera
  • Click - Select unit/city
  • M - Move selected unit
  • B - Build city (Settler)
  • F - Fortify unit
  • Space / Enter - End turn
  • Escape - Deselect
  • 1-5 - Build units in city
